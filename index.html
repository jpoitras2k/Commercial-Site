MOLD REMEDIATION

Like asbestos, mould is increasingly recognized as a health concern and a major problem in buildings today.

If you detect any mold damage to any area of your home or business please call the team that can help. Mold can cause serious health problems, don't put your family or employee's health in danger. Your home or business can quickly become infested with mold with the introduction of a water source, like a roof or plumbing leaking.
Mold can be found almost anywhere; it can grow on virtually any organic substance, as long as moisture and oxygen are present. There is mold that can grow on wood, paper,carpet, foods, and insulation. When excessive moisture accumulates in buildings or on building materials, mold growth will often occur, particularly if the moisture problem remains undiscovered or unaddressed.
